Ground Water Potential Zone Map

Utilized the Multi-Criteria Decision-Making (MCDM) process to delineate groundwater potential zones across Bankura District. The analysis integrated various thematic layers such as slope, land use/land cover, soil type, and rainfall, weighted based on their influence on groundwater recharge. The resulting map classified the district into four categories: Very Low, Low, Moderate, and High potential zones, highlighting critical areas for groundwater management. This study aids sustainable water resource planning and management.
